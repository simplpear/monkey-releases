How It Works

Three steps. No setup. No accounts.

1

Just type

MonKey lives in your menu bar and quietly monitors your keyboard. It detects layout mismatches in real time as you type.

2

Select text

For translation, grammar or AI actions — select the text you need. Layout autocorrection doesn't need selection — MonKey remembers what you just typed.

3

Press a hotkey

One hotkey — done. MonKey fixes your layout, translates or checks grammar instantly. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

Private by Default

The free tier is 100% offline. Your keystrokes never leave your Mac. Cloud features are opt-in and only send the text you explicitly select.

Context-Aware

MonKey tracks sentence context and learns from your corrections. The more you use it, the smarter it gets — fewer unnecessary fixes, more accurate layout detection.

Your Data Stays Yours

MonKey reads your keyboard to fix layout errors — not to spy on you.
Here's how we keep your data safe.

No Keystroke Logging

Keystrokes are processed in memory and immediately discarded. Nothing is written to disk in release builds.

Free Tier Is Offline

Zero network connections for text processing. Free tier works entirely on your Mac with no internet required.

Zero Telemetry

No analytics, no tracking, no crash reporting. We have no way of knowing how you use the app.

Cloud Is Opt-In

Premium AI features only send text you explicitly select. No background data collection, ever.

App Store Reviewed

Every update is reviewed by Apple before release. The app runs in a macOS sandbox with minimal permissions.

Verify It Yourself

Use Lulu or Little Snitch to monitor network traffic. Use Objective-See ReiKey to inspect keyboard taps.
